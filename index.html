Hypothesis and Visualization Approach

I hypothesize that there is a positive correlation between the overall vehicle population in Singapore and COE premiums. As the vehicle population increases, the demand for vehicles intensifies, which may drive up the COE bidding prices. In addition, the regulatory changes implemented in May 2022—which altered the eligibility criteria for certain COE categories— might have introduced a notable shift in the premium trends.

For this visualization, I use two datasets: COE Bidding Results / Prices (with monthly data in the format "10-Jan" representing 2010-01) and Annual Motor Vehicle Population by Vehicle Type (with yearly data from 2005 to 2025). The chart features a dual-axis time series: the left y-axis shows COE premiums (red line), and the right y-axis shows the aggregated vehicle population (green line). A vertical annotation at May 2022 highlights the regulatory change.
